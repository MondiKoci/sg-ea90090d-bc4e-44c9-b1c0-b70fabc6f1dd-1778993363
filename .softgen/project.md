# Dental Tourism Clinic Website

## Vision
A premium dental tourism platform connecting international patients with high-quality dental care. The site bridges medical credibility with destination appeal, positioning the clinic as a trusted choice for patients seeking affordable, world-class dental treatments abroad.

**Target audience:** International patients (primarily North America/Europe) seeking cosmetic and restorative dental procedures at significant cost savings without compromising quality.

## Design
Primary aesthetic: Precision medical facility meets boutique hospitality — Scandinavian dental clinic crossed with upscale travel concierge.

**Color System:**
- `--primary: 210 29% 20%` (deep slate #243444 — clinic brand color, medical trust)
- `--accent: 43 74% 66%` (champagne gold #E8C468 — premium touch)
- `--background: 40 33% 94%` (warm cream #F7F5F0 — approachable warmth)
- `--foreground: 210 29% 15%` (rich slate — high contrast text)
- `--muted: 40 15% 85%` (warm grey — subtle backgrounds)
- `--card: 0 0% 100%` (pure white — elevated surfaces)
- `--border: 40 20% 88%` (soft warm border)

**Typography:**
- Headings: Sora (geometric precision, medical professionalism)
- Body: Source Serif 4 (editorial warmth, readability)
- All weights: 400, 600, 700

**Style Direction:** Clean lines, generous spacing, elevated card surfaces with subtle shadows, warm cream backgrounds (not stark white), champagne gold accents on CTAs and highlights. Confident medical authority balanced with welcoming hospitality.

## Features
- Hero with trust signals (certifications, patient count, savings highlight)
- Treatment showcase (implants, veneers, full mouth reconstruction)
- Why Choose Us (cost comparison, quality credentials, care package)
- Patient testimonials with before/after visuals
- Destination highlights (location, accommodation, travel support)
- Contact/consultation booking flow
